Hi, my name is Drew Long. I'm a UX designer and researcher with over ten years' experience in K-12 education and qualitative research.

I am a UX Designer and Researcher focused on the front end of product design: generative research, defining the problem and experience design. My goal is to find an agency role that engages with diverse projects and stakeholders and thrives on collaboration across teams.

My Work

Fotonovelas for Health Literacy

This project is a prototype for a bilingual, mobile-first website that transforms a printed educational resource into an interactive digital one for low literacy users. As UX designer, I oversaw the entire project lifecycle, including a uniquely challenging user research phase, remote moderated usability testing and a final high fidelity prototype.

User Research, User Interviews, Surveys, User Journeys, User Flows, Wireframing, Prototyping, Preference Testing, Usability Testing, Responsive Design
